SPORTS CLUB

Volleyball club

The volleyball club at Vit Pune encourages students to participate in volleyball and
develop their teamwork and athletic skills. It is part of the college’s sports activities managed by the physical education department.
The campus has a volleyball court and sports ground where students can practice and play matches regularly.
The club organizes practice sessions, friendly games, and intra-college tournaments among different departments.
Students who perform well can represent VIT Pune in inter-college competitions and university tournaments.
The club promotes physical fitness, coordination, and team spirit among players. Sports equipment like volleyballs
and nets are provided by the institute for training. Selection for the official volleyball team is usually done through trials conducted during the academic year.
Many students participate in volleyball during annual sports events and college festivals. Overall, the volleyball club plays an important role in promoting sports culture at VIT Pune. 🏐

Key Activities

  • 🏐 Organizing intra-college volleyball tournaments and friendly matches

  • 👥 Managing and training official VIT Pune volleyball teams for inter-college competitions

  • 🎯 Conducting regular practice sessions to improve skills, fitness, and teamwork

  • 🏅 Selection and coaching support for competitive volleyball events

  • 🤝 Collaborating with sports clubs and external organizations for tournaments and events

  • 📢 Promoting volleyball culture, sportsmanship, and active participation among students
