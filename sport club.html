SPORTS CLUB

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune maintains a robust sports culture through
its dedicated sports facilities and year-round athletic programs that unite students from all engineering branches.
The campus features a cricket ground, football pitch, basketball and volleyball courts, indoor badminton hall,
gymkhana, and athletics track, hosting regular inter-departmental tournaments across multiple disciplines.
Annual sports fests showcase competitive leagues in cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis,
and athletics, fostering team spirit and physical fitness essential for engineering students' well-being.

Students participate in Pune University inter-collegiate championships, state-level competitions,
and intra-college knockout tournaments, earning sports certificates valuable for academic records and placements.
Leadership opportunities arise through sports council roles in event organization, team captaincy, and tournament management.
Regular fitness drives, yoga sessions, and health awareness programs complement competitive sports, promoting work-life balance.
These activities, peaking during even semesters' sports weeks, develop discipline, resilience, and time management skills crucial for
holistic student development at VIT Pune.
