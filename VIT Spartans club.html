SPORTS CLUB

VIT Spartans club

The VIT Spartans Sports Council at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology,
Pune is the official body that promotes sports and athletic culture on campus.
It organizes and manages various sports teams, fitness events, tournaments, and inter-college competitions,
encouraging students to participate in physical activities, develop teamwork, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
The council supports budding athletes, fosters sportsmanship, and helps students balance academics with extracurricular excellence.

Key Activities

  • 🏃‍♂️ Organizing intra-college and inter-college sports tournaments

  • ⚽ Managing and training official VIT Spartans teams for games like football, cricket, basketball, etc.

  • 🏅 Selection & coaching support for competitive athletes

  • 🤝 Collaborating with sports clubs and external organizations

  • 💪 Promoting health, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students
