TECHNICAL CLUB

TRF VIT

TRF - The Robotics Forum is the official and one of the oldest robotics clubs at
Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. It brings together students from various engineering
disciplines who are passionate about robotics and automation.

Key Activities

  • 🤖Designing and building innovative robotics projects

  • 🛠Conducting workshops on Arduino, embedded systems, CAD, and programming

  • 🏆 Participating in national competitions like ABU Robocon, e-Yantra & SIH

  • 📚 Providing technical training and hands-on learning

  • 👥 Encouraging teamwork, mentorship, and peer learning

  • 🎤 Organizing technical events, demos, and knowledge-sharing sessions
