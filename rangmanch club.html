RANGMANCH CLUB

The Rangmanch Club at VIT Pune is dedicated to promoting drama, theater, and performing arts among students. The club provides a platform for students to explore acting, stagecraft, scriptwriting, and
production skills. Members actively participate in organizing stage plays, skits, and cultural
performances within the college as well as in inter-college events. Rangmanch Club encourages creativity,
teamwork, and confidence by giving students opportunities to perform in front of audiences.
The club also conducts workshops and training sessions to help students enhance their acting,
dialogue delivery, and stage presence. Participation in the club helps students develop leadership,
communication, and event management skills. Students from all departments are welcome to join and contribute to theatrical projects.
Overall, Rangmanch Club plays a key role in nurturing performing arts and cultural expression at VIT Pune. 🎭

Key Activities

  • 🎭 Organizing and performing stage plays and skits
  • 🏆 Conducting workshops on acting, scriptwriting, and stagecraft
  • 🤝 Promoting teamwork and collaboration among members
  • 🎤 Providing opportunities for students to perform on stage
  • 🌟 Encouraging creativity, expression, and confidence
  • 🏆 Participating in inter-college drama and cultural events
  • 📢 Managing stage setups, props, and event organization
