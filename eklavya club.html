EKLAVYA CLUB

The Eklavya Club at VIT Pune focuses on sports and physical fitness, aiming to encourage students
to lead an active and healthy lifestyle. The club provides a platform for students to participate in various sports,
outdoor activities, and fitness programs. Members practice regularly, participate in inter-department and
inter-college tournaments, and organize sports events on campus. The club promotes teamwork,
discipline, and leadership through sports activities. Students also get opportunities to learn new sports,
improve their skills, and gain exposure to competitive events. Eklavya Club collaborates with the
sports department and other clubs to conduct training sessions and fitness workshops.
Participation in the club helps students maintain physical fitness while developing confidence and strategic thinking.
The club welcomes students from all departments who are passionate about sports. Overall, Eklavya Club plays an
important role in building a strong sports culture and promoting healthy lifestyles at VIT Pune. 🏅

Key Activities

  • 🏅 Organizing and participating in sports tournaments and competitions
  • 🤝 Promoting teamwork, discipline, and leadership through sports
  • 🎯 Conducting practice sessions and skill development activities
  • 🏋️ Organizing fitness programs and workshops
  • 🌟 Encouraging students to learn new sports and improve skills
  • 📢 Collaborating with sports department for events and activities
  • 🎉 Hosting inter-department and campus sports events
