TECHNICAL CLUB

CSI VIT

It's the official student chapter of the Computer Society of India at VIT Pune.
This club brings together students passionate about computers, coding, and technology.
Its main goal is to help members build real tech skills through workshops, hackathons, seminars,
industry visits, and tech sessions, where students learn from professionals and hands-on events.
The club also organizes coding competitions, networking events, and career-oriented talks to bridge the gap between academics and real-world tech experience.

Key Activities

  • Competitive Programming Workshops: Problem-solving strategies, speed coding for LeetCode/Codeforces
  • Android Development Bootcamps: Java + Firebase app building
  • Guest Speaker Sessions: Industry experts from Citi Bank, Zensar, Cyber Unbound
  • Hands-on Coding Practice: Online repositories, take-home challenges
