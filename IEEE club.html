TECHNICAL CLUB

IEEE club

IEEE Student Branch VIT Pune at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune
is the official student chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)
that aims to foster technical innovation, learning, and excellence among engineering students.
The branch organizes a variety of hands-on workshops, tech talks, hackathons, competitions,
and community events that help students enhance their technical skills, collaborate on projects,
stay updated with emerging technologies, and build a strong professional network.

Key Activities

  • 🤖 Conducting technical workshops (TechLoops) on programming and engineering concepts

  • 💡 Organizing hackathons and coding challenges for practical learning

  • 🧠Organizing hackathons and coding challenges for practical learning

  • 🤝 Collaboration and mentoring among members

  • 🌐 Providing community networking and professional development opportunities
