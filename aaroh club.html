CULTURAL CLUB

AAROH CLUB

The Aaroh Club at VIT Pune is dedicated to promoting cultural, artistic, and creative activities among students.
The club provides a platform for students to explore their talents in music, dance, drama, and other performing arts.
Members actively participate in organizing cultural events, performances, and competitions within the college and at inter-college festivals.
The club encourages teamwork, creativity, and confidence by allowing students to showcase their skills in front of audiences. Aaroh Club also conducts workshops and training sessions to help students improve their artistic abilities. Participation in the
club helps students develop stage presence, leadership, and event management skills. Students from all departments are welcome
to join and contribute their ideas and talents. Overall, Aaroh Club plays a significant role in fostering cultural awareness and artistic growth at VIT Pune. 🎶

Key Activities

  • 🎶 Organizing music, dance, and drama performances
  • 🎭 Conducting cultural events and competitions
  • 🤝 Promoting teamwork and collaboration among members
  • 🗣️ Hosting workshops to enhance artistic skills
  • 🌟 Encouraging creativity and confidence in students
  • 🏆 Participating in inter-college cultural festivals
  • 🎤 Providing opportunities for students to showcase talents
