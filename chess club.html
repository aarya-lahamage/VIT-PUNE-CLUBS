Chess club

The Chess Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology,
Pune is a student organization dedicated to promoting the game of chess
among students of all skill levels. The club encourages strategic thinking,
concentration, and friendly competition by organizing regular chess sessions,
casual matches, and structured tournaments. It provides a platform where beginners can learn
the game and experienced players can sharpen their skills, fostering a community of chess enthusiasts on campus.

Key Activities

  • ♟️ Organizing regular chess meetups and practice sessions

  • 🏆 Hosting intra-college chess tournaments and competitions

  • 🎓 Beginners’ learning sessions and strategy discussions

  • 🧠 Promoting strategic thinking and analytical skills

  • 🤝 Friendly matches and chess challenges among students
