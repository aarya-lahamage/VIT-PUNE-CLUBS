CO-CURRICULAR CLUB

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune supports vibrant co-curricular activities that enhance
technical and professional skills for engineering students across all branches through hands-on projects, competitions,
and industry exposure. These include entrepreneurship workshops fostering startup ideas and pitch competitions,
literary events promoting debate, quizzing, and creative writing, plus photography sessions with skill-building and exhibitions.
Sustainability initiatives feature tree plantation drives and green audits, while social service involves community outreach,
blood donation camps, and rural tech adoption programs.

Co-curricular clubs at VIT Pune are diverse student-led organizations fostering skills beyond academics,
including technical, cultural, and social development. They promote creativity through events like workshops and open mics,
and enhance technical expertise in areas like robotics, coding, and data science. These clubs aim for holistic development and teamwork
