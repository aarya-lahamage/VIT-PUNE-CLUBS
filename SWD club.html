Social Welfare and Devolopment Committee VIT

The SWD Club at VIT Pune focuses on student welfare and development by
organizing activities that support students academically, socially, and personally.
The club works to create a positive and supportive environment on campus where students can share ideas,
solve problems, and grow together. Members of the SWD Club help in conducting awareness programs, workshops,
and student support initiatives throughout the academic year. The club also collaborates with other student groups
to organize events that benefit the student community. Through its activities, the SWD Club promotes teamwork,
responsibility, and leadership among students. It plays an important role in improving communication between students
nd the college administration. Students involved in the club gain valuable experience in
organizing events and helping their peers. The club encourages participation from students of different
departments and backgrounds. By working together, members contribute to improving the overall student experience at VIT Pune.
Overall, the SWD Club helps build a supportive and active student community.

Key Activities

  • 🤝 Organizing student welfare and support programs
  • 📢 Conducting awareness campaigns and social initiatives
  • 🎓 Arranging workshops, seminars, and development activities
  • 👥 Helping students with guidance and peer support
  • 🌱 Promoting personal growth and leadership among students
  • 🎉 Supporting and coordinating campus events
  • 🏫 Working with administration to improve student life
