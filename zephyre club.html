ZEPHYRE CLUB

The Zephyre Club at VIT Pune is focused on promoting innovation, creativity, and event management skills
among students. The club provides a platform for students to organize, plan, and execute college events,
technical fests, and cultural programs. Members actively participate in coordinating events, managing logistics,
and ensuring smooth execution of activities on campus. Zephyre Club encourages teamwork, leadership,
and problem-solving skills through practical involvement in event management. The club also conducts
workshops and training sessions to help students learn about organizing large-scale events and handling responsibilities.
Participation in the club allows students to develop communication, organizational, and planning skills.
Students from all departments are welcome to join and contribute their ideas and energy. Overall,
Zephyre Club plays a vital role in nurturing leadership, coordination, and creative problem-solving among students at VIT Pune. 🎉

Key Activities

  • 🎉 Organizing and managing college events, festivals, and programs
  • 🤝 Coordinating with students, faculty, and other clubs
  • 🗓️ Planning and executing event logistics and schedules
  • 🌟 Promoting teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills
  • 🎓 Conducting workshops and training sessions on event management
  • 📢 Managing promotions, announcements, and publicity for events
  • 🏆 Participating in and organizing inter-college events and competitions
  • 📢 Managing stage setups, props, and event organization
