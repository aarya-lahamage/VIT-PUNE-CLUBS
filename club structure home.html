Our Club Structure

About Our Club Structure

Our club is organized to provide every member an opportunity to contribute,
learn, and grow. The structure ensures smooth coordination of activities,
effective communication, and active participation in events and projects.,

Club Leadership

President

Leads the club, manages overall activities, and represents the club.

Vice President

Assists the President, coordinates between teams, and manages events.

Core Teams

Event Management Team

Plans and executes workshops, seminars, and competitions.

Technical Team

Handles projects, coding sessions, and technical support.

Design & Media Team

Creates posters, social media content, and event documentation.

Marketing & Outreach Team

Promotes club events and manages communication with students and sponsors.

Volunteer Team

Supports all club activities and helps in smooth event execution.

Membership Team

Manages new member registrations, orientation, and member engagement.

Team Workflow

The club functions through a coordinated workflow where each team reports to the
core leadership, collaborates with other teams, and ensures successful completion
of all activities. Members are encouraged to participate in multiple teams and
contribute according to their interests and skills.

Together we organize, innovate, and achieve!
