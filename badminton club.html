SPORTS CLUB

Badminton club

The Badminton Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology,
Pune is a student-led sports club that brings together badminton enthusiasts to play,
train, and compete. It promotes physical fitness, skill development, and friendly competition
among students of all levels. The club organizes regular practice sessions, internal matches,
and tournaments that help members improve their techniques, enjoy teamwork, and build a strong sporting community on campus..

Key Activities

  • ♟️ Organizing regular meetups and practice sessions

  • 🏆 Hosting intra-college competitions

  • 🎓 Beginners learning sessions and strategy discussions

  • 🤝 Friendly matches and challenges among students
